🍽️ Our Story


⏳ 2017

Asylum Cafe first opened its doors on June 14, 2017, in a cozy corner of the city. Born from a love of good coffee, great food, and even better company, it started as a tiny passion project with just six tables, one espresso machine, and a dream. Over the years, we’ve grown into a beloved neighborhood spot where regulars know the staff by name and newcomers feel right at home.

🎯 Purpose

The name Asylum isn’t a warning — it’s a promise. We’re a safe haven for caffeine addicts, dessert lovers, and anyone escaping from the chaos of daily life. Our goal? To serve food so good it feels a little bit crazy not to order seconds, and coffee strong enough to make Monday mornings almost tolerable.

🧑‍🍳 Staff

Our kitchen is led by a team of chefs with over 30 years of combined culinary experience. Each has formal training from renowned institutions. Our baristas are certified coffee experts, trained in latte art and espresso brewing techniques to ensure every cup meets our high standards. Together, we’re here to make sure your visit is unforgettable — for all the right reasons.
